About the Convention

Since its inception, IIT Kanpur has been at the forefront of many technological and scientific advancements in the past and keeps on making strides with its exceptional research culture. This stature could not have been achieved without the expertise of our faculty, and an equal dedication and excellence of our students. With this thought at heart, SRC is a kind effort to bring to light the exceptional research contributions being made by students, over the nation.

SRC aims to foster student-based research and create a platform for young researchers to meet and exchange ideas. Being the first of its kind, SRC brings together the best college students from all over the country to present their innovations and experience the current research culture across the country.

Apart from the student conferences, SRC encompasses talks, panel discussions, workshops, academic competitions, technical exhibitions, and sessions covering various aspects of research and its implications in the growth and development of Industry, Society and Economy of the nation.

RISE

This year, at SRC'20:
Let's Research in Space Exploration !

This decade has undoubtedly been the decade of Space Exploration. From the launch of the Curiosity rover by NASA in 2011 to the launching of 104 satellites in a single mission by ISRO in 2017, be it the development of the Starship or the revolutionary reusable rockets by SpaceX, the space feats attained in the last ten years make the domain of Space Research a non-negotiable and an integral part of what we envision for the future of Science, Technology and us. Hence, SRC'20, through it's theme and stage, calls for budding researchers and scientists to "R.i.S.E".

S p a R E

  • The exponential progress in the field of Space Research directly points out the necessity to create awareness and motivate the students to pursue research with applications attesting to the domain of Space Sciences.

  • With this thought at heart, SRC'20 proudly presents the newest addition to our list of enlightening and impactful theme talks-SpaRE (Space and Research)

  • Experts from various domains of space research - from Rocket Propulsion and Launch Vehicle Designs to exo-planetary explorations, shall talk and interact with the students on different aspects of space research, it's scope, potential opportunities and enlighten the audience on how these awesome and successful space missions are planned and executed!
